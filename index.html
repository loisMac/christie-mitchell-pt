Hello, I'm Christie!

My pronouns are she/her.

Glasgow-based personal trainer obsessed with movement and lifting heavy things. My love affair with lifting started about 9 years ago. Learning to lift weights changed the way I viewed my body — I started to appreciate my body for all the cool shit that it could do and worried less and less about what it looked like.

I'm passionate about helping people get comfortable in a gym environment, knowing that they have every right to be there and to take up space. The gym is for EVERY SINGLE ONE OF US.

I owe a lot to training. It constantly reminds me that I can do hard things and that my body is so much more than how it looks.
